3eesho App

Overview

In June of 2014, I enrolled in a 10 week iOS development course through Codepath. Over the course of the 10 week period, I learned the fundamentals of iOS development and gained a solid understanding of Objective-C. The final output of this class was a fully functional health and fitness Application built for the non-profit organiztion, 3eesho. This project was by no means a singular effort and was built alongside Amie Kweon, Chirag Dave, and Joseph Lee

My Role

I led design and built out many of the interactions in the application. Most of my early work was to make sure we had a cohesive app that made sense and flowed well.
