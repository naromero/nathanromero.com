Get to know me.

Who is this guy anyways?

Well, I'm sure that by now you've figured that I am Nathan Romero. I'm a 6'4", brown haired, hazel-eyed graphic designer based out of San Francisco, CA. I recently graduated from UC San Diego with a degree in Cognitive Science and have jumped into employment rather quickly after graduating.

Although I worked my butt off for four years in college, a career in the field cognitive science still doesn't seem like a good fit for me. Design has been a hobby of mine since I was a freshman in high school, assuming a secondary role behind education. It wasn't until I landed a job at a design studio my junior year of college, that I considered turning my hobby into a viable career choice. During my time at this job, I really began to hone my skills as a designer. I was given the opportunity to design something new everyday and picked up design theory along the way. I regard my time at this design studio as some of the most transformative years of my life.

Tell me something interesting. Hobbies?

As far as hobbies go, the list is pretty short at the moment. Graphic design has been something I've done in my free time, after homework was done and tests were studied for. Now that midterms are a thing of the past and my 9-5 is spent designing, I'm left with a lot of free time. During there hours, you can probably find me reading up on HTML/CSS, spending time with my girlfriend, or at a local card room taking some suckers money.
