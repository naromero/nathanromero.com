My name is Nathan Romero. I'm a San Francisco based designer who specializes in design for the web. From wireframes and user testing, to pixel-hinting and writing code, I've had my hands in nearly every part of the design process. I have a holistic understanding of what it takes to get a project out of the door, and my passion for detail ensures the end result fully realizes the initial vision. As you’ll see, I like working with big type and vibrant colors, and it’s with this style that I aim to bring a wholly unique experience to the web.
