A Responsive Marketplace

Overview

An exploration into how AppDirect's white-label e-commerce marketplaces could be redesigned in a responsive way.

My Role

In early 2013, I was tasked with the role of taking the existing layout of our marketplaces and redesign them in a way that was responsive down to a mobile-friendly size. One of the most challening aspects of this project was figuring out how to simplify and reorganize information, as screen space became sparse. A general approach that I took in solving problems like this was taking inventory of elements existed in a certain layout, and prioritzing them in order of importance. From there it was typically much earier to order elements on a page and give visual priority to elements that called for it.
